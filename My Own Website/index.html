Inspiration

Mittens, the chess bot introduced by Chess.com, became a viral sensation for its unique blend of charm and skill. Unlike traditional chess bots, Mittens was designed with a playful and somewhat mischievous personality, featuring witty and often taunting responses during games. Despite its cute cat avatar and lighthearted demeanor, Mittens showcased formidable chess abilities, capable of challenging even experienced players. This creative approach not only made chess more engaging for players of all levels but also demonstrated how AI can blend entertainment with education, sparking renewed interest in the game.
